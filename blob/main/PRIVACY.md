# Privacy Policy for Pocket Hub

**Last Updated: January 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **Pocket Hub** handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
Pocket Hub is designed as a **local-first** application. 
* **Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal financial data to external servers.
* **Financial Records:** All information regarding your income, expenses, and loans is stored directly on your device using a local database.
* **Biometric Data:** If you enable FaceID or Fingerprint locking, this process is handled by your device's operating system. Pocket Hub never sees or stores your biometric data.

## 2. Data Storage
All data is stored in a private database on your mobile device. If you delete the app, all data stored within the app will be permanently deleted unless you have made a manual backup.

## 3. Permissions
Pocket Hub may request the following permissions:
* **Biometrics (FaceID/Fingerprint):** To secure your app from unauthorized access.
* **Storage:** To export reports (like PDF or CSV) to your device.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not share your data with third parties. Since your data never leaves your device, there is no risk of your financial history being sold or shared.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
